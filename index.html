Colombia

Built on the same property as the first church of Bogota, built in 1539, the Catedral Primada de Colombia opened its doors to worshippers in 1823. Within its walls are many historic treasures, including the tomb of Gonzalo Jiménez de Quesada, Bogota’s founder.

Iceland

Hallgrimskirkja church is the landmark symbol of Reykjavik, Iceland. Visible from almost any point in the city, Hallgrimskirkja Church sits at the top of Skolavordustigur Street. The church was designed by Gudjon Samuelsson, one of Iceland’s most famous and beloved architects.

Portugal

Belem Tower from Lisbon, Portugal. Construction started in 1514, and finished five years later, with the tower named Castelo de São Vicente de Belém (The Castle of Saint Vincent of Bethlehem). A year-long full restoration was finished in early 1998, leaving Belém Tower as it appears today. It was declared one of the “Seven Wonders of Portugal” in 2007.

source: https://www.tripsavvy.com/belem-tower-lisbon-portugal-guide-4154619
